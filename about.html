About Eminem 

Marshall Mathers or better known as Eminem was born on the 17th of October 1972 who began his rap career by rapping about his abusive family at the age of 14 in clubs in hometown Detriot, Michigan. He quit school in the ninth grade to persue his rap career. He released his first album infinite in 1996 and the following year came second in the freestyle olympics in Los Angelas caught thte attention of famous rapper and producer Dr Dre, Dre signed Eminem onto his label N.W.A and brought Eminem to the spotlight after the first release of the alter ego's Slim Shady's release of "The Slim Shady LP" in 1999 and the rest is history and you can find out more in Albums & Awards.
